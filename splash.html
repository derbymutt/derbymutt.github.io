PROPOSAL
I have created 1,100 graphics in a tile sheet for modular use.
All are scratch made at 24x24 pixels with a 1 pixel division and the entire sheet is offset by 1 pixel in the x and y directions for optimal viewing ease.
I've tried to accommodate and predict every possible outcome for tile shapes and designs to fit together properly, in a modular format.
Each main tile in any sub-set has the same location in the sub-set as the equivelent tile in every other sub-set.
The engine, thruster, and lazer tiles are located only in the sub-sets of the core element that they represent.
